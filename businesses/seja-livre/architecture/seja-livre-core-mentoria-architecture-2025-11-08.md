## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### Blueprint Overview

This landing page architecture is designed to convert **solution-aware, burned-out female entrepreneurs** (€30-50k/month revenue) into qualified applicants for Seja Livre's Core Mentoria program. The page leads visitors through an emotional journey from crisis recognition to hope, belief, and action—culminating in a discovery call application (pricing revealed in sales conversation).

### Strategic Positioning

**Core Differentiation:** "The Only Business Mentorship Designed to Liberate You FROM Your Success"

- **vs. Camila Vidal (Moving Girls/MDA):** We own "systems for liberation" vs. her "mindset transformation"
- **vs. Bettina Rudolph:** We own "holistic business freedom" vs. her "digital marketing tactics"
- **vs. BORA Mulheres:** We serve established businesses in crisis vs. early-stage entrepreneurs

### Key Architecture Decisions

1. **Juçanã's Bio Positioned Early (Section 2)** - Establishes trust immediately since she's the primary selling point
2. **No Pricing on Landing Page** - Investment revealed during discovery call for maximum value-building
3. **11-Section Long-Form Architecture** - Justified by high-ticket positioning (R$9,970) and need for extensive proof
4. **Application Funnel Model** - Landing page → Application → Discovery Call → Enrollment
5. **Crisis-First Messaging** - Leads with burnout intervention vs. generic growth promises

### Expected Performance

**Target Conversion Metrics:**
- **Landing Page → Application:** 20-30% (solution-aware + crisis urgency)
- **Application → Discovery Call:** 65-75% (qualified leads only)
- **Discovery Call → Enrollment:** 25-35% (personal relationship + value-building)
- **Overall Conversion:** 3-7% (cold traffic to enrollment)

**Benchmark:** High-ticket coaching industry standard is 2-5% overall; we expect higher due to crisis positioning + strong guarantee.
