## Quick Start: The 3-Question Method

If you only have 30 seconds, ask these three questions:

1. **"How many people are you ordering for?"**
   - 4-6+ people = Weekend Family Heroes
   - 1-2 people = Spontaneous Romantics
   - 3-4 people + familiar = Weekend Regulars

2. **"Is this your first time ordering from us?"**
   - Yes = New customer (provide excellent first impression)
   - No = Returning customer (check if they're a Regular)

3. **"When would you like delivery?"**
   - Today/tonight = High urgency, likely Spontaneous Romantics
   - This weekend = Planning ahead, likely Weekend Family Heroes
   - "My usual time" = Definitely Weekend Regular

## Red Flags & Misidentification

### Signs You've Misidentified the Persona:

**Thought Weekend Family Heroes, but actually:**
- Ordering only 1-2 pizzas = Probably couple or small family
- No kids mentioned = Might be Spontaneous Romantics
- Very frequent orders = Might be becoming Weekend Regular

**Thought Spontaneous Romantics, but actually:**
- Order is for 4+ people = Weekend Family Heroes
- Mentions kids = Weekend Family Heroes
- "Same as last time" = Weekend Regular

**Thought Weekend Regular, but actually:**
- First time customer = Not regular yet (provide excellent experience)
- Different order than usual = Note the change, confirm

### When Unsure:

1. Ask clarifying questions
2. Provide excellent service regardless
3. Note persona for future reference
4. Adjust next time based on patterns
