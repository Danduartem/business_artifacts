## ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes to Avoid

**Mistake 1:** Constantly changing menu or discontinuing their favorite pizzas.
*Why it fails:* Weeknight Loyalists order the same items repeatedly. If portuguesa or calabresa changes or disappears, you've broken the trust. Consistency includes menu stability.

**Mistake 2:** Complicated ordering processes or pushing app downloads.
*Why it fails:* This persona values simple, proven methods. Phone and WhatsApp work perfectly. Forcing them to download apps, create accounts, or navigate complex systems creates friction and abandonment.

**Mistake 3:** Inconsistent quality or delivery times.
*Why it fails:* This is THE cardinal sin for Weeknight Loyalists. They choose you FOR consistency. One disappointing experience can end years of loyalty. Quality must be absolutely reliable.

**Mistake 4:** Youth-focused, trendy marketing that ignores their demographic.
*Why it fails:* If all your content is aimed at 20-year-olds with TikTok trends, 45-year-old professionals won't feel like your brand is for them. You must speak to established adults too.

**Mistake 5:** Failing to recognize and appreciate repeat customers.
*Why it fails:* When Sr. Roberto orders for the 40th time and you treat him like a stranger, you miss a powerful loyalty moment. "Your usual, Sr. Roberto?" builds unbreakable bonds.
