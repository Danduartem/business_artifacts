### Sales & Qualification

**If ordering for 4+ people:** Likely Weekend Family Heroes
- Ask about kids, special occasion, weekend plans
- Recommend: Portuguesa + Calabresa (safe, kid-friendly)
- Emphasize: Portion size, family satisfaction

**If ordering for 1-2 people:** Likely Spontaneous Romantics
- Quick, efficient service (they're hungry now)
- Recommend: Bacon Special, interesting options
- Emphasize: Fast delivery, perfect for two

**If repeat customer with pattern:** Likely Weekend Regular
- **Use their name** ("Sr. Roberto, your usual?")
- Offer same order they always get
- Emphasize: Consistency, appreciation

### Content Creation

**For Families:** Relatable parent humor, permission to take shortcuts, family happiness moments, weekend scenarios

**For Young Couples:** Aesthetic food photography, date night vibes, spontaneous fun, Instagram-worthy presentations

**For Regulars:** Appreciation content, consistency messaging, community connection, local business pride
