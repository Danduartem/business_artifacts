## âš ï¸ Common Mistakes to Avoid

**Mistake 1:** Messaging focused on individual indulgence rather than family solutions.
*Why it fails:* Weekend Family Heroes aren't ordering for themselves - they're solving a household need. "Treat yourself" messaging misses the mark; "Feed your family easily" hits it.

**Mistake 2:** Ignoring kids' preferences or assuming sophisticated tastes.
*Why it fails:* If the kids reject the pizza, this persona views it as a failed order regardless of quality. Classic, kid-friendly flavors must be prominently featured.

**Mistake 3:** Complex ordering processes or unclear portion guidance.
*Why it fails:* This persona is exhausted and decision-fatigued. They need clarity: "This feeds 4-6 people, order now, delivered at 7pm." Complexity creates abandonment.

**Mistake 4:** Weekday-focused promotions and messaging.
*Why it fails:* Weekend Family Heroes order on weekends. Your best promotions should hit Thursday evening through Saturday afternoon when they're planning/ordering.

**Mistake 5:** Price-only value propositions without quality assurance.
*Why it fails:* While budget-conscious, this persona won't risk disappointing their family with low-quality food to save R$10. Value = quality at fair price, not cheapest option.
