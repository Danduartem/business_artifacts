## âš ï¸ Common Mistakes to Avoid

**Mistake 1:** Family-focused messaging and bulk portion emphasis.
*Why it fails:* This persona is ordering for 1-2 people. "Feed the whole family" messaging is irrelevant and actually alienating. They need "perfect for two" or "ideal for solo indulgence."

**Mistake 2:** Only offering safe, traditional flavors without interesting options.
*Why it fails:* Unlike families worried about picky kids, Spontaneous Romantics want to try interesting flavors. Bacon specials, creative combinations, premium options appeal to them.

**Mistake 3:** Requiring advance planning or pre-scheduling orders.
*Why it fails:* They order spontaneously when craving hits. Same-day, quick turnaround is essential. "Order 24 hours ahead" loses this persona entirely.

**Mistake 4:** Formal, corporate messaging tone.
*Why it fails:* They respond to casual, relatable, authentic communication. Instagram captions should feel like a friend, not a corporation. Use their language, their humor, their vibe.

**Mistake 5:** Ignoring social media visual appeal.
*Why it fails:* This persona lives on Instagram. If your pizza doesn't photograph well or your feed isn't aesthetic, you're invisible to them. Visual quality is non-negotiable.
