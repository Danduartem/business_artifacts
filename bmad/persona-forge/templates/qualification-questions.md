## Tips for Effective Qualification

1. **Be conversational** - Weave questions naturally into dialogue
2. **Listen actively** - Their unprompted comments often reveal more than direct answers
3. **Observe patterns** - Look for consistent themes across multiple answers
4. **Trust your gut** - If something feels off, probe deeper
5. **Adapt in real-time** - Adjust your questions based on what you're learning
6. **Document quickly** - Add notes to CRM immediately after the call

## Common Misidentification Patterns

### Mistaking Solution Aware for Most Aware
**Warning signs:** They ask lots of questions, seem hesitant, need more information
**Reality:** They're comparing, not ready to buy
**Fix:** Back up one level, provide more education

### Mistaking Unaware for Problem Aware
**Warning signs:** They don't engage with problem-focused content
**Reality:** They don't see the problem yet
**Fix:** Focus on awareness-building, not problem validation

### Confusing Personas
**Warning signs:** Messaging doesn't resonate, unexpected objections
**Reality:** Wrong persona match
**Fix:** Re-qualify with focused questions on demographics and psychographics
