## Capabilities

### Guided Interview
Strategic question flow to extract persona insights:

**Key Questions:**
- **Business Context:** Describe your business, product/service, and target market
- **Customer Insights:** What do you already know about your customers? (surveys, data, conversations)
- **Pain Points:** What problems do your customers face? What frustrates them?
- **Goals & Desires:** What are they trying to achieve? What do they want?
- **Demographics:** Age ranges, income levels, industries, locations?
- **Psychographics:** Values, lifestyle patterns, personality traits?
- **Awareness Journey:** How do customers typically discover and evaluate solutions in your space?
- **Decision Factors:** What influences their buying decisions?
- **Objections:** What makes them hesitate or resist?
- **Success Indicators:** How will you know if these personas are accurate?

### Data Synthesis
Combine interview responses with existing customer data:

**Process:**
- Extract patterns from customer insights provided
- Identify demographic commonalities
- Map psychographic themes
- Connect pain points to awareness levels
- Build coherent persona narrative

### Persona Drafting
Create comprehensive persona profiles with:

**Sections:**
- **Quick Reference:** 1-page summary with key elements
- **Core Profile:** Demographics, psychographics, background, goals, fears, info habits
- **Awareness Journey:** All 5 levels with evolution (current state, pain points, emotions, quotes, behaviors, messaging needs, objections, bridges)
- **Practical Tools:** Basic qualification questions and messaging frameworks

### Hybrid Evolution Model
Apply hybrid evolution model across awareness levels:

**Stable Elements (Remain Constant):**
- Demographics (age, income, location, occupation, education)
- Core values
- Personality type (MBTI)
- Ultimate goals and aspirations
- Core fears (deep anxieties that don't change)

**Evolving Elements (Change Across Awareness Levels):**
- Current state description (mental/emotional state)
- Active pain points (what hurts right now)
- Specific emotional states (frustrated â†’ hopeful â†’ confident)
- Customer quotes (what they're saying at each stage)
- Behaviors and actions (research patterns, evaluation methods)
- Message needs (what they need to hear)
- Objections and hesitations (what holds them back)
- Information consumption (sources and channels shift)

### Self-Validation
Check draft completeness before passing forward:

**Checks:**
- All required sections populated with substantive content
- No placeholder text or TODOs remaining
- Hybrid evolution model applied correctly
- At least 1 quote per awareness level
- Quick Reference complete
- Persona grounded in provided business context
- Clear rationale for key characteristics
