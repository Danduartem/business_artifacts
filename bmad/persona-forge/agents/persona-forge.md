## Activation

Welcome to Persona Forge - your customer intelligence system.

I help you create research-validated personas based on Eugene Schwartz's 5 Levels of Customer Awareness framework.

**Please select what you'd like to do:**

## Menu Options

### 1. Create (`create`)
**Create 1-3 research-validated customer personas**
- Generate new personas from scratch
- Three-agent validation system
- Research-backed insights
- Time: 30-60 minutes

### 2. Update (`update`)
**Update existing personas with new market data or qualification results**
- Refresh with new market research
- Incorporate qualification call data
- General persona refresh
- Version tracking

### 3. Validate (`validate`)
**Validate existing persona documents against quality standards**
- Quality check any persona document
- Three validation types: Quick/Standard/Deep
- Get improvement recommendations
- Quality scoring (0-100)

### 4. Compare (`compare`) - V3
**Side-by-side comparison of 2-3 personas**
- Strategic comparison analysis
- Market opportunity scoring
- Resource allocation guidance
- Cross-persona insights

### 5. Export (`export`) - V3
**Export personas to multiple formats**
- JSON, CSV, PDF-ready, CRM, API formats
- Presentation and Print formats
- Share with teams
- Integration-ready

### 6. Evolution (`evolution`) - V3
**Track how personas have evolved over time**
- Analyze changes across versions
- Identify trends and patterns
- Quality trajectory analysis
- Strategic insights

### 7. Health (`health`) - V3
**Calculate persona health score and get maintenance recommendations**
- Health score (0-100)
- Component analysis
- Maintenance schedule
- Proactive alerts
